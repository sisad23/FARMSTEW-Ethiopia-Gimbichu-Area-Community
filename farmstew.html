FARMSTEW ETHIOPIA GIMBICHU AREA COMMUNITY.

INTRODUCTION ABOUT THE COMMINITY

The community is established in July, 2024(Hamile,2016) after being well trained by FARMSTEW Ethiopia which is being lead by Parousia Ministry ,Ethiopia.The members of the community have deep knowledge on: modern farming, positive attitude, how to take rest, how to feed themeselves and their family, general sanitation ,temperance, enterprise and the use of water(including water treatments). Currently the Community members are working on general trade and on modern farmig to be financially developed. But in near future the community is planning to give the tranning for other people who need to get knowledge. Beside this, the members (except some)are well trained to treat the sick who are suffering different kinds of disease naturally by using simple herbs these God have given for the sake his children.

THE COMMUNITY'S MISSION, VISION & GOAL

Our community is driven by a clear mission to deliver innovative and sustainable solutions that improve lives and create lasting value for our customers and communities. Guided by our vision to be a global leader in our community, we strive to inspire trust, foster growth, and set new standards of excellence. Our primary goal is to continuously enhance the quality of life and services while embracing integrity, innovation, and customer satisfaction at every step. Through collaboration, commitment, and forward-thinking strategies, we aim to shape a better future for all the people.

The Community's Mission

The Community's mission is to train families to break free from extreme poverty so they can experience the abundant life offered by Jesus.This can be done by improving health and wellbeing of rural areas.

The Community's Vision

The Community's vision is inspired by Jesus' desire that all “may have life and have it more abundantly”

The Community's Goals

The Community's goal is for mothers and fathers to develop the capacity to transform their family's lives and their communities, offering a hopeful witness to the world that points to the True Source of abundant life. Jesus offers this life in John 10:10, saying, “I have come that they may have life and that they may have it more abundantly.”

The Departmental Division in the Community.

For the sake of community's goal and for the fair distribution of work load, the total work of the community is divided into 7(seven) wings.These are:-The Spritual cases wing, Development wing, Health and healing wing, Charity cases wing, Social affairs wing, Song related cases wing and Media, Communication and technology wing. In these seven groups the full members are grouped and working actively.

Spritual Cases Department

This department will adjust programs for: prayer, outreach and for Bible study. And in full members seminars and conferances that can include other people it is the department's responsibility to adjust everything(including who is going to give service).

Financial Development Department

Since there will be no movements without money, the department is the backbone of the community. So it is the responsibility of this department to gather market information, to create business chains and to bring new ideas of business to run.

Social Cases Department

The responsibility of this department is to deal about social cases that will happen in the community members. Finally the department conclude on the cases by supporting the on the community manual. Then will bring to members to announce how to participate on the case.

Health and Healing Department

The main aim of this department is teaching the surrounding community on what the disease is, how will someone be affected by disease(reason of being sick), and how can he/she treat the case in home by simple remedies.The department is also resiponsible health expos at community confrences.This can be done by communicating with the committee of the FARMSTEW Community.
